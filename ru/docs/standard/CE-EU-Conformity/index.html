CE European Conformity

The CE (Conformité Européenne) mark is a mandatory safety mark for products entering the EU market.

Meaning of the CE Mark​

The CE mark indicates that a product meets the essential requirements of relevant EU directives and can circulate freely within EU member states and the European Economic Area (EEA).

CE Certification Path for Heiman Products​

Heiman smoke/CO alarms meet CE requirements through the following routes:

CPR Regulation (EU No. 305/2011)​

The Construction Products Regulation requires fire alarm products to undergo performance stability assessment:

  • Applicable standards: EN 14604 (smoke), EN 50291 (CO)
  • Assessment body: TÜV Rheinland and other notified bodies
  • Certification level: DoP (Declaration of Performance) + CE mark

RED Directive (2014/53/EU)​

The Radio Equipment Directive requires wireless devices:

  • Covers wireless modules such as Zigbee, Z-Wave, Wi-Fi, Thread
  • Test items: Electromagnetic Compatibility (EMC), Radio Frequency (RF), SAR, Safety

EMC Directive (2014/30/EU)​

The Electromagnetic Compatibility Directive ensures that equipment does not cause interference to other electronic devices.

CE Marked Products​

All Heiman products sold to the EU market bear the CE mark, including:

  • HS1SA-E / HS3SA (Zigbee smoke alarm)
  • HS1CA-E / C1 series (Zigbee CO alarm)
  • HS1SA-M / HS1CA-M (Matter over Thread)
  • HS1MS / HS1DS and the full range of Zigbee sensors
