Other Certifications

Q-Label Quality Mark​

Q-Label is a quality certification mark for the European smart home industry, launched by the Q-Lab Alliance (now part of the Zigbee Alliance / CSA Connectivity Standards Alliance).

ItemDescription
Scope of ApplicationZigbee device interoperability certification
Certification ContentCompatibility of devices with other brands' Zigbee gateways/controllers
SignificanceEnsures cross-brand interoperability, reducing the risk of "not working after purchase"

Heiman Zigbee products are Q-Label certified, ensuring compatibility with the following platforms:

  • Amazon Echo Plus / Echo Show (built-in Zigbee)
  • Philips Hue Bridge
  • SmartThings Hub
  • Other Q-Label certified gateways

Red Dot Design Award​

The Heiman HS1SA series smoke alarm has won the Red Dot Design Award, reflecting Heiman's strength in product design.

ISO 9001 Quality Management System​

Heiman has passed ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management System certification, ensuring full-process quality control from R&D and production to after-sales service.
